F.A.Q.
 
Why do you spell "phone" with an "f"?
Just to answer this once and for all, the term "OHRRPGCEFMF" does not mean "OHRRPGCE For Mobile Phones." That's a handy mnemonic device for remembering all the letters, but, in fact, the actual acronym is the egregious:
Official Hamster Republic Role Playing Game Creation Engine
Haecity
Reduction
Remix
Project, a:
Gratis,
Clean-room,
Extensible, &
Fully
Mobile
Framework
 
Is that a backronym?
Nope, sorry! This was the intended name since the project began, for two reasons. First, it honors the (standard) OHRRPGCE's author's odd beliefs about acronyms. Second, the OHRRPGCE FMF's goals include not replacing the OHRRPGCE, and there is power in a name.
This acronym is also not a recursive acronym, nor is it a retronym, an antonym, or an isonym (clearly). Please stop impressing me with your word-ology.
